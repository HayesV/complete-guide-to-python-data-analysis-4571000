Temperature Anomaly
- difference of the average temperature in a year (average of the average min and average max) and the average tempatrue, averaged over the decade inclusive of 1945-1955

Compute the anomaly for all years between 1880 and 2023, rounded to 2 decimals

Paramters
- allyears : 1 144x365 NumPy record array with TMIN and TMAX fields containing min and max temps from 1880 to 2023 and days of year 1:365
- reference years correspond to 65:75

Solution

import NumPy
old Average = 0.5 * np.mean(allyears[65:76]['TMIN]) + np.mean(allyears[65:76]['TMAX'])

anomaly = 0.5 * (np.mean(allyears['TMIN']) + np.mean(allyears['TMAX'])) - old average

return np.round(anomaly,2)
